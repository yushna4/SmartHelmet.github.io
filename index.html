Smart Medicine Dispenser

Smart Medicine Dispenser is an automatic medication dispenser that organizes, schedules and delivers patient medication without manual human intervention. These devices comes with built-in alarms, lights and send notifications to their designated guardians after medication is taken.

Results and Ongoing Progress:

The resultant smart medication dispensing system yielded remarkable outcomes and continues to exhibit promise:

-Helps patients who are empowered with timely reminders through the alarm sound from the device.

-Gives information through app about whether the medicine has been taken by the patients or not.

-Gives medicine to patients according to the time set on the device.
